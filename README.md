## 📖 What is FisiCollab?

FisiCollab is a web-based platform designed to help students and teachers share academic resources in a simple and safe way. It works on any Windows computer through your browser. The system is built with care to protect your data and make teamwork easy. You can upload files, find study material, and work with classmates smoothly.

This platform uses well-known technologies like Node.js, Express, and MySQL behind the scenes. You don't need to know programming to use it. Just open the app, log in, and start sharing or finding resources.

## 📂 How to Use FisiCollab

Once logged in, you can access several key features.

### Upload and Share Files

- Click the "Upload" button to add new documents or study materials.  
- Select one or more files from your computer. Supported formats include PDF, Word documents, images, and videos.  
- Add a title and optional description to help others find your files.  
- Choose whether the file is public or limited to certain users or groups.  
- Press "Save" to complete the upload.  

Your resources will appear in your library and can be accessed by collaborators.

### Search Resources

- Use the search bar at the top to find shared resources.  
- Filter results by file type, course, or user.  
- Click any item to open its details or download it to your device.  

### Manage Your Profile and Settings

- Open the profile menu to edit your details or change your password.  
- Adjust notification preferences to control email alerts.  
- Log out securely when finished.
